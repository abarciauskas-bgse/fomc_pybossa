Redundancy in FOMC minutes

Two statements are redundant when the second conveys the same message as the first, irrespective of persons or places. In this evaluation, ignore dissimilar people and places.

The application is really simple. It displays two statements extracted from FOMC minutes and asks: Are these 2 statements redundant?

You will have 3 possible answers:

  • Yes,
  • No, and
  • I don't know
